about me

I am a student of the class of 2021 in the Informatics Engineering Study Program, and I am from Baturiti, an area located in Bedugul. I chose this major because of my deep interest in technology and innovation, as well as my desire to contribute to developing technology-based solutions in the future. My origin from Baturiti gives me a unique perspective that combines Bali's natural beauty with a passion for pursuing knowledge and skills in the field of information technology

I have a hobby in logo design and gardening, which is in harmony with my background as a resident of Baturiti, an area where the majority of the population is farmers. Both are my passions, as logo design allows me to channel my creativity, while gardening gives me a direct connection to nature and agriculture. I have aspirations to apply technology wisely in the agricultural sector, hoping to help improve the efficiency and productivity of farmers in my area and throughout Indonesia. By combining my expertise in the field of information technology and my love for agriculture, I have the ambition to create innovative solutions that benefit the agricultural sector and society
